Fizzbuzz Application

Traditionally this application is applied to numbers in the range of 1-100 but feel free to choose any starting and ending number to determine your own table.

Next choose one number and it's multiples to be replaced by the word "Fizz" and another to be replaced by "Buzz".

"FizzBuzz"
will automatically replace the numbers which are multiples of both the first and second values you choose to
"Fizz"
and
"Buzz"
.
